Meet Kevin Ng

Every day is a chance to become better

Kevin Ng is a certified CrossFit Level 1 Coach & fitness guru, NASM CPT, former DI swimmer at the University of Rhode Island, Deepend Fitness Tier 2 Instructor, entrepreneur, & health advocate. Kevin has been swimming since he was six years old and was a member of the 2011 ECC Men's University Of Rhode Island Club Championship Swim Team. Kevin found a deeper understanding of teamwork, dedication, and sacrifice from an early age. ​ Kevin's passion for health and fitness continued to develop beyond his swimming career when he became more involved in personal training and group fitness. By building life-changing relationships with his clients, he improved physically and mentally. To further help his clients, Kevin decided to pursue further education to become a CrossFit Instructor. ​ In 2019, Kevin took a leap of faith and moved from New Jersey to California to further his passion for an active and healthy lifestyle. He discovered Deep End Fitness, a low-impact water fitness program designed to improve water confidence, build efficient breathing, and boost mental fortitude; It was the perfect marriage of swimming and fitness combined. Kevin is now a certified Tier 2 Instructor & Head Coach with Deep End Fitness, Team Irvine. With knowledge and expertise from his training background and personal athletic experiences, Kevin enjoys helping athletes be more confident, move better, and live healthier. He assists them in taking those steps towards changing their lives one breath at a time.
